WHO WE ARE

Mwelase Thobs Construction & Projects is an emerging wholly women & black-owned company committed to the development of South Africa and its future. We are a dynamic company where innovation and lateral thinking is promoted. Mwelase was created with the intention of venturing into the construction industry in which the previously disadvantaged did not take part.

The Founder and Director, Ms Precious Monwadibe Ratlou, is a young, dynamic black woman with a passion for community-oriented growth and development. Her passsion for empowering aspirant young women is the key differentiator for her drive to succeed

Our Goals

Our Goals are informed by a clear Vision, Mission and Stategic Objectives

Strategic Intent

Vision

  • A leading African civil and building construction company of choice, intent on providing quality services that conform to global industry best practices and standards.

Mission

  • To deliver innovative services enabled by superior workmanship in the civil and building construction sector throughout whilst maintaining excellent quality control.
  • To foster long-term, sustainable professional relationship with our clients based on trust, performance and client satisfaction.
  • To contribute to economic growth of South Africa through strategic partnerships and job creation interventions for the communities in which we operate.

Strategic Objectives

  • Establish and build good quality buildings for schools, offices and homes.
  • Construct roads that are safe and of the best quality possible
  • Minimise the rate of unemployment by creating job opportunities. Changing the mind set of workers by actively involving them in the running of the company in the form of mentorships.
  • Empower the disadvantaged with business skills.
